Hi
If you came here it's because you want to know more about us, right?
Well, sit down, here comes the story...
We opened our first store in 2011, in the city of Nova Friburgo, a small town located in the countryside
in the mountains of the state of Rio de Janeiro. In that same year our city had gone through a great catastrophe of rains
but with a lot of faith we managed to open our store.

Our first location was a very small store, which was more like a house that the person rented on a street that only had stores,
it was in a well hidden alley, but God honored us and we managed to make our nest.

In less than a year we got our first location with the "face" of a store, it was on a very nice street in the lingerie pole
in Nova Friburgo.
We stayed in this store for 6 years and we were very successful and we greatly increased our clientele. This store was very good
but there was more waiting for us.

The year of 2017 arrived with everything and we got a great opportunity to take one of the best spots of all the streets, we made our
change and we were very grateful for everything we were able to do, but you know what the life of an entrepreneur is like, we can never stop!
In 2020 the pandemic attacked us a lot with the closing of trade for months, but we held on and in 2021 we entered
in online sales through some marketplaces. It was an excellent move for us as we now had two stores: online and
physics, to serve our customers.

Today we are super focused on our online sales, wanting to focus even more on our mission from the beginning,
offer quality underwear fashion at a price that everyone can afford, not to mention that service that we never fail.
If you have read this far, I hope you enjoyed our story and we look forward to seeing you in our physical store or in our online store 💗
