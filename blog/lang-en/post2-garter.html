The secrets of the garter belt, do you know?

When it comes to lingerie and sensuality, the garter belt is always there!
Symbol of feminine lust and daring, the garter belt stirs the imagination of men and women and is a fundamental suggestion
for those who really want to surprise in moments of intimacy!
But do you know how the garter belt came about? And better than that: know how to use it to your advantage?

Garter belt: from French cabarets to your wardrobe
The garter belt was first used by French cabaret dancers in the 19th century.
At that time, girls used to wear a model of socks known as stockings, which covered almost
the entire leg and stopped at mid-thigh. As the dancers moved their legs a lot, the garter belt came up with the purpose of
keep her stockings in place and, little by little, left the cabarets to compose the look of women all over the world.

Why wear a garter belt?
Because of its history and the way it is present in people's fantasies,
it is a key piece to compose a femme fatale look. It even seems like a contradiction, because at the same time that
partially covers the skin, brings an expression of sensuality to the woman's body that no other garment is able to bring.
If you want to explore your potential in intimacy and show mastery and security over your own body, start now
choosing the model that best suits you!

What are the garter belt models?
Remember: in addition to being sexy, it is essential that you feel comfortable!
Models that have a wider front are generally more comfortable to wear and offer good coverage.
You can also choose between straps with 4 or 6 alloys and, of course, between the most varied colors.
To bet on the tradition, my tip is the black or red garter belt, to help you become even more sassy!
