Panties with gym clothes. What's the best option?

Some Brazilians opt for the gym, others prefer to do it at home, how to go hiking and running outdoors.
Regardless of the choice of each one, a question is very common among women:
which lingerie or panties to wear with gym clothes?

Panties with gym clothes: Be careful with the seams
The vast majority of women look for panties of comfortable models and materials for use with gym clothes,
especially during physical exercise.

The first choice of the female audience for this scenario is usually light fabric pieces, such as cotton or microfiber .
It is worth remembering that the greatest attention that should be given when making this decision is to opt for sewing-free panties.
That's because these models will not show the underwear mark on top of the gym clothes.

Panties with gym clothes: Thong panties?
Despite not being unanimous, a quite significant portion of the female population prefers the use of thong underwear
with gym clothes.

Without pretending to present magical solutions, or absolute and indisputable tips, it is essential that every woman has freedom
to choose the pieces you want from your wardrobe to use, even during gymnastics and physical activities.

However, it is worth noting that the choice of thong with the use of gym clothes is an option that should be considered by women.
This is because, when using gym clothes, it is assumed that the woman will do physical activities and, in this way,
it is possible that the thong panties hurt you due to the movements made.

As mentioned before, regardless of the material of the panties, it is interesting to look for models seamless,
as these panties are usually more comfortable and do not leave marks on clothes.

Why wear panties with gym clothes?
Just as there are some women who opt for lightweight fabric pieces and others opt for thong models, some women
prefer not to wear panties with gym clothes.

It is known that the complete non-use of underwear is not advised by health professionals, especially among women
because their genitalia are open and therefore more susceptible to the entry of organisms that can cause infections or allergies.

However, there is no evidence to show that not wearing panties for certain periods of time leads to health problems
On the contrary, several women report that this is the most comfortable way to do the exercises, in addition to not
having witnessed any kind of health problem arising from the habit of not wearing panties under gym clothes.

What is the best?
For the answer to be coherent, it is necessary that the question is asked to the greatest expert in the subject: you.

Only you can decide how best to handle panties and gym clothes. Do you prefer comfortable models?
Don't give up thongs panties? Do you think panties only get in the way of gym clothes and prefer not to use them?
Only you will be able to answer properly which option is best for you and your body. What do you think about it?
