The footless stocking is a wardrobe classic and an integral part of some of today's most sophisticated and seductive looks.
The footless stocking first appeared centuries ago and in some cases prevalent in winter.
With perfect conditions to warm the body and help improve blood circulation, footless stockings were common between
the 17th and 18th century.

However, this piece was rejected by society between 1800 and early 1900. In fact, the footless stocking became
seen as an excessively sensual piece, which made it synonymous with lewdness and vulgarity. For this reason, for more than one
century the call girls were the only ones to socially use these stockings.

The power of feminism and women's desire to be free, without restrictions, was crucial to saving this piece
about the mid-twentieth century, stockings have been part of the feminine look again, this time with more force
and with other functions, besides just warming up the female body in winter.

The biggest advantage of this "sock", nowadays, is the sophistication and elegance that the footless stocking brings to any look of which
it is a part.
Models and occasions to use, creating breathtaking looks: here you'll find everything about footless stocking.

Another great time to wear the footless stocking and get the look right is that special intimate night. Assemble sexy looks with

Without fear of making mistakes: Composing looks with the sock ⅞

For couples who like to play and let their imaginations run wild, wearing costumes with the footless stocking is the best alternative.

Stocking models
There are footless stocking of all types and for all tastes. But the most popular models are:

  • Prints
  • Standardized
  • Plain
  • Lace
  • Draw

Models with prints and patterns are more suitable for thin and tall women. That's because, with the legs
long and thin, the stocking composes the look with greater sobriety.

In the case of short women or with more rounded legs, it is best to use smooth models.
This indication is made because prints and patterns usually create the impression of reduced height and increased width,
I mean, if you're short or chubby, the last thing you want is to look even shorter or even chubby, right?

Lacy stockings, fishnet stockings or any other details should be used with great care. This finish refers to the
corset stockings and lingerie in general. In that sense, make use of this footless stocking model only if your goal is
conquer the cat, ok?

Concluding
The footless stocking has several models and finishes and is suitable for all women who want to compose elegant looks,
especially in intimate moments or at night social events.

Wear footless stocking with skirts or dresses and a thick coat on top, such as wool or leather. When your goal is to conquer
the cat, however, you can associate the sock with a corset or sensual costume, for example.

Don't forget basic care, okay? Delicate items like a sock footless stocking to be hand washed after a few minutes
immersed in water. Avoid prolonged exposure to the sun, drying it in shady and ventilated places or in the early sun
day or late afternoon.
